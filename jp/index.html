OUR STORY

Our team comes with the experience and knowledge

Lenef is a business and technology consulting firm headquartered in Hanoi, Vietnam. Founded by a group of experts, our firm has been providing cutting edge engineering solutions and business consultancy, helping worldwide customers untangle their issues. We pride ourselves in the quality of our work and constantly seek new ways to improve our know-how and capabilities.

We integrate our knowledge and proper experiences into providing the most innovative and optimal solutions for delivering best services.

We aim to be the best consulting firm with precisely standardized procedures in which we utilize to work with clients to map their journey and make it a reality.

Services

Our Best Services

Not only do we provide innovative solutions to all your business and technology problems, but we also help make your brilliant ideas reality.

Offshore Development Center

We offer ODC (Offshore Development Center) services to help clients rapidly scale up or down their development workforce, cut the development costs, and reduce time to market. It is the almighty tool that can serve the most complex needs and requirements of companies of all shapes and sizes.

Desktop Application Development

We offer custom Desktop solutions that cater to your every need, Design, application development services, to maintenance, and support.

Web Application Development

Our web developers are keen on making websites as gorgeous as they are responsive. We are experienced in building web applications on various business domains, including e-Commerce, e-Learning and so on.

Mobile Application Development

We make it our mission to make transformative and friendly mobile experiences that delight your customers. We have built thousands of native, hybrid, and cross-platform apps that run on all major operating systems, including iOS and Android.

Embedded System Design and Development

We have the expertise to quickly develop complex Embedded hardware based on the most popular microcontroller & FPGA. The hardware that we develop is efficient & cost-effective. We augment our Embedded hardware development with software/firmware development as well.
